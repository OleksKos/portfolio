Six steps to succeed goal

Customer Profile

Customer portrait (customer profile) is a collective image of the target audience. It describes a typical user of a product or service. This is the image of a real customer for whom your product is a solution to a problem. It is very important to know your target audience to create the correct content and figure up excellent Unique Selling Proposition.

Create USP

A unique selling proposition (UPS) is the characteristics of a brand or its products that distinguish them from competitors and make them stand out in the marketplace. These characteristics must be valuable to the target audience. Simply put, the USP is the answer to the question "Why should you be the one to contact?

Write a content

Based on previous information about your brand and having UPS we can strat making a content which is apeal your clients and customer and convience them buy your products and services. The content includes images, videos, text etc. Thought many important thing we must implement to the site, but content is a King of your brand and site.

Design

After that, we proceed to the design of the site. First, we develop the structure of the site, then we create a mockup of the site and coordinate it with the customer. Then we proceed to the graphic design of your site.

Programming

Then we proceed directly to the development of the site. Depending on the complexity and goals of the client, this can be HTML, CSS and JavaScript. For large sites, we use WordPress and the WooCommers, using the Divi and Elementor visual constructors.

Testing

Testing is the conclusion milestone. We test every aspect of the site including responcibility, responciveness, loading speed, working contact forms and quises. We set up hosting and domain for you and make them working.
